About HopeBridge

The Problem

In floods and earthquakes, people donate random items, but affected areas often lack the actual essentials needed for recovery.

Our Solution

A real-time donation-matching platform where NGOs update live needs (food, clothes, medicines) and donors see exactly what is required.

Our Services

Live Need Dashboard

Real-time updates of what disaster-affected areas actually need

Donor Matching

Smart system matches donors to nearest NGOs with specific needs

Maps Integration

Interactive maps showing disaster zones and relief centers

Impact Tracking

Monitor the real impact of donations on affected communities
